### Hard-won DSL gotchas (already cost debugging time)
- Selector alternation is `|` not comma: `build|check:` (comma = `sub, level`).
- A rule/arm body is EITHER a flat pipeline OR a cascade — never a flat op THEN a
  `match`/`if`. Push shared ops into each arm (macros-in-arms is fine).
- Guards only see `exit ok|failed` (not specific codes) + `level x` + flag atoms.
- `or-shell:` runs against RAW (over-prune recovery), not the pruned stream.
